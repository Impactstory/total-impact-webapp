Impactstory advisors

Do you love Impactstory and want to tell others about us? Are you already an advocate who wants cool perks like free premium features and high fives from our team? Become an Impactstory Advisor! We’re now accepting applications to be part of a select group of researchers and librarians who believe in Impactstory to help spread the word!

Advisor responsibilities

  • Maintain a top-notch Impactstory profile
  • Invite friends and colleagues to try out Impactstory
  • Help us understand the needs of researchers in your field or region
  • Spread the word locally by hanging up our cool new posters
  • Stay on top of the latest Impactstory news
  • Host brown bag lunches and presentations on Impactstory in your school or library
  • Engage your university's administration on how Impactstory can help its faculty
  • Connect Impactstory to the rest of your online life--link to your profile from your Twitter bio, Facebook page, lab website, and anywhere else you can!
  • Tell us your Impactstory success stories, in promotions, grants, tenure, and beyond.

Your Benefits

  • Satisfaction that you’re helping to change academic research evaluation for the better!
  • Free Impactstory swag--t-shirts, stickers, and more!
  • Premium Impactstory features (coming soon!) for free
  • A shiny Advisor badge for your Impactstory profile and website
  • Have pizza and drinks on us when you give a demo or talk at your institution
  • A community of like-minded, cutting-edge Advisors
  • High fives from our team members, whenever you see them in person!
